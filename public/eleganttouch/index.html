Our Story

A Neighborhood Salon With a Big Heart

Elegant Touch has been a cornerstone of Brookhaven for decades. We're not just a salon — we're the place where families come together, where stylists remember your name, your color formula, and exactly how you like your blowout.

Our team brings over 25 years of industry knowledge and stays current with ongoing education in the latest styles, techniques, and products. From your first visit to your hundredth, you'll always leave looking — and feeling — your best.

We specialize in color correction, which means even the trickiest hair challenges are in good hands here.
