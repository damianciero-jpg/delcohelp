## Why It Matters

Your members already struggle to find resources. They don't know which pantries are open, which benefits they qualify for, or who to call in a crisis. When they do find out, it's often too late or too confusing to act on.

A branded app from your organization changes that. It puts your name on a tool that genuinely helps people — and it keeps your community connected to your mission every day.

---

## What's Included in Every Plan

Every white-label app includes:

- Your organization's name, logo colors, address, and contact information
- Your mission statement or scripture verse on the home screen
- Full resource finder for Delaware County (pantries, assistance programs, legal aid)
- Emergency hotlines and one-tap crisis calling
- Benefits navigator with eligibility quiz and document checklists
- SMS text-back service for members without smartphones
- 4-language support (English, Spanish, Vietnamese, Chinese)
- Offline access — works without internet
- Crowdsourced pantry status and inventory
- Family profile, saved resources, and resource sharing
- Crisis Escape Plan (private, device-only safety planning)
- "I'm Going Tonight" visit tracker with reminders
- "I Found Help Here" impact confirmation

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do my members need to download anything?**
No. They open a link on their phone, tap "Add to Home Screen," and it works like a native app — offline-capable, with notifications.

**Is any of my members' data stored on your servers?**
No. All user data (saved resources, family profile, crisis plan) is stored only on the user's own device. We collect anonymous analytics only.

**Can I add my own resources to the directory?**
Yes. We can add any local resource — your food pantry, your counseling referral, your clothing drive — to your app's directory.

**Can we switch tiers later?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**What if we already have a website?**
The app works alongside your website. Most churches link to it from their homepage and weekly bulletin.

**Is there a contract?**
No long-term contract required. Month-to-month on all plans.
